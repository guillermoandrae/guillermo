Intro

Guillermo Andrae Fisher has been working on the Web for almost twenty years in several capacities, many of which are detailed on LinkedIn. He is currently a Senior Engineering Manager on the University team at Handshake. He is also the founder of 757ColorCoded, an organization focussed on helping people of color achieve careers in technology. Guillermo is a Christian, husband, father of four, a continuous delivery enthusiast, writer, and a fan of very silly comedy.

Projects & Decks

He aggregates his social media activity1 and makes the sum of it available for public consumption through a Web API and a simple search UI. There are a few interesting repositories in existence at his GitHub account—including the code for this website2. He is known to occasionally speak at Meetups, colleges, and conferences; you can find the decks for some of his talks at Slideshare.
